Petty Cash Management For Accountants

Spare your accountants from laborious double entries for expenses !

Very nice and easy module for managing Petty Cash expense !

Features

  1. Comprehensive petty cash dashboard.
  2. Petty cash request to account manager.
  3. Cash dispatch By account manager.
  4. Cash reconciliation for cash dispatch and petty cash expense.
  5. Manage journal entries for petty cash.
  6. Real-time effect on the finacial report such as balance sheet and profit-loss.
  7. Analytics account and tags for assigning cost centre to petty cash expenses.
  8. Document attachment and document viewer feature, to attach bill or receipt of petty cash expenses !
