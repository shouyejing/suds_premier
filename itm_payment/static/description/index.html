Payment Management For Internal Transfer.

Very nice and easy module for managing internal transfer payment !

By default Odoo comes with one payment type for internal transfer of fundsthat is journal to journal .
This module introduce two more options.
1) Journal to Account transfer
2) Account to Journal transfer

Features

  1. Manage Different type of internal transfer !
  2. Simple and easy way to manage Payment!
  3. Single click entry posting.
